Cheap-Cheap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Cheap-Cheap is a grocery price-comparison app for South African retailers. It is designed to need no account and no personal data.

Information we collect

None. The app does not require you to sign up or log in. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers. There is no analytics or advertising SDK in the app.

What the app does with data

The app fetches publicly available, aggregated retail product prices from our backend and displays them on your device. Your search terms and shopping list stay on your device and are not sent to us or any third party.

Third-party services

The app retrieves price data from our hosted database (Supabase). These requests contain only the product query needed to return prices — no personal data.

Children

The app is rated for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised policy at this URL.
